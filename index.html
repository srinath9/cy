Our Mission


"Change Yourself" is a student-run social enterprise which aims at involving students in creating positive social impact. Mission . To provide a platform to the energetic youth of India through which they can bring a change in the society and improve themselves simultaneously. . To create young leaders with vision and energy for the nation as well as themselves. . To trendify the education of India by involving students in this field. . To spread awareness among the people about the general world issues related to economy, science, law, environment, history, sports, polity and business. . To ensure that any idea which can create a significant impact does not go waste just because that person finds himself alone in his pursuit.

Description This organisation is run by students of some of the best institutions in the country like IITs, AIIMS, NLIUs, NITs and other institutions of high repute. It centrally works from IIT Kharagpur. However, it is open to people from all regions and age.
