About AISAT

AISAT(All India Student Aptitude Test) was started last year with a vision of helping the students in identifing their natural tendencies through a systematic eveluation test of their aptitude

The exam is conducted in Two Levels:-

    For Students from 6th to 10th- It will be a one hour exam and will have questions from Verbal Reaoning, Legal Reasoning, Quantitaive aptitude ,General Awarness and Scientific Aptitude.
    The aim of this test will be to help in indentifying the career option which will be the best for them to opt according to their aptitude.

    For Students from 11th to 12th- It will be a Three hour exam and will have questions on entirely PCM/PCB. The aim of this exam will be-

  • To act as a bridge between their orutine main stream syllabus and the competitive exams they are going to give in the future.

  • To inculcate scientific aptitude and critical thinking among the students to prepare them for all types of exams they are going to face after their 10+2.

  • To let them know their strengths and weakness and the areas they need to work upon.

  • To give them a feel of the questions whichare asked in the competitive exams and how they differ from those asked in Board examinations.

      • For 6th to 10th Class students, the fee of AISAT is Rs.100 and for 11th and 12th Class Students, the fee if AISAT is Rs. 200.

      A salient feature of AISAT is that we will provide an analysis Report to the candidates on the basiss of their differnt abilities.

      The questions will stress more on reasoning and critical thinking rather than subject knowledge
